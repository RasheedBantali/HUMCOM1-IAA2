SWEET

The most basic definition is that sweet food has the flavor or taste of sugar or honey, while savory food is not sweet, but rather full-flavored and sometimes spicy. Sweet and savory foods often complement each other at a dinner.

What?

Food is any substance consumed to provide nutritional support for an organism. Food is usually of plant, animal, or fungal origin, and contains essential nutrients, such as carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, or minerals.

Where?

All food comes from plants, even animals depend on plants. Hence, we obtain food from plants directly or indirectly.

How?

Start searching on the internet or travel around the world to find out the different kinds of foods that people make.
