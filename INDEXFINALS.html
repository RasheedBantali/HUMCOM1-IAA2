1. MT YANGBEW

Location: Mt. Jumbo Trail, Tawang La Trinidad, Benguet

Description:

A flat peak Mountain range in the heart of La Trinidad that offers you a panoramic view of the valley. You could also get a glimpse of the mountain ranges surrounding it. The top of the mountain is covered with grasses which are ideal for cow or horse grazing. Here you can take a horse riding activity on your day tour or do a day or night camping. Just make sure you get your umbrellas with you as it’s an open field. The trail can be accessed from Pines Park or on the Shilan route.


2. STARWBERRY FARM

Location: Strawberry Farm Km6. La Trinidad, Benguet

Description:

The cream of the crop of La Trinidad Valley. You could take a walking tour onto the farm with different vegetables that are cultivated in the area. Do strawberry handpicking and shop for local harvests, souvenirs, artisans’ works, and even ornamental plants too!


3. MT KALUGONG AND CULTURAL PARK

Location: Tawang La Trinidad, Benguet

Description:

If you love a bit more thrill, head on to this rocky mountain range. The high sharp and uneven structures of the rocks are challenging and it’s not for the faint-hearted. Gear up when you are climbing up the area. But you may also skip that activity by just having a picnic or do camping on their picnic ground. There are also available local accommodations and even a cozy shop in this cultural park.


4. LIVING GIFTS NURSERY

Location: Alno La Trinidad, Benguet

Description:

An oasis of varieties of Cacti grown in their large greenhouse and connected greenhouse tunnels. For green thumb folks, this is something you shouldn’t miss. You can spot plenty of species of cactus from huge columnar ones to tiny cacti buttons. The main entrance is beautifully adorned with sets of cacti and even succulents. Getting wonderful shots from it is out of the question. This is a place your eyes feast on their numerous cacti collections. You could also purchase some at a reasonable price!


5. BELL CHURCH

Location: Km. 3 La Trinidad, Benguet

Description:

A small Buddhist temple nesting onto the slope of the mountain. Getting here is just 10 minutes ride from Baguio City- if not a traffic jam. The Bell Church is open to everyone. Just observe silence and abide by their house rules when you are on a visit.
